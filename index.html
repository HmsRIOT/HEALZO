Our Services

Healzo offers comprehensive home healthcare services designed for your well-being.

Post-Operative Care

  • Wound dressing and suture removal
  • Drain and catheter care
  • Pain management
  • Mobility support and physiotherapy coordination
  • Daily nursing follow-up (as prescribed)

Elderly Care Services

  • Medication reminders and monitoring
  • Hygiene and grooming assistance
  • Vital signs monitoring (BP, sugar, pulse, SpO2)
  • Fall risk assessment and safety support
  • Mental well-being check-ins

Chronic Disease Management

  • Diabetes monitoring and insulin administration
  • Hypertension care
  • Stroke recovery assistance
  • COPD/Asthma support
  • Regular vitals charting and escalation if needed

Home Nursing Services

  • Part-time or full-time nurse attendance
  • Female nurse availability upon request
  • Trained attendants for palliative or geriatric support
  • Nurse visits for injections, IV fluids, dressings

Doctor On Call (Phase 2)

  • Video consultations for minor ailments
  • Home doctor visits if required (coordinated with local partners)
  • Second opinion service for ongoing treatments

Specialty Add-ons (Future Scope)

  • Physiotherapy at home
  • Lab sample collection
  • Nutritionist consultation
  • Remote health monitoring with wearable integration

About Healzo

Healzo is dedicated to revolutionizing health and wellness through technology and personalized care. Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health journeys with accessible, reliable, and innovative tools.

Founded on the principles of empathy and excellence, we strive to build a community where everyone can achieve optimal well-being. From preventative care to chronic disease management, Healzo is your trusted partner every step of the way.

We believe in a holistic approach, combining cutting-edge research with compassionate human connection to deliver results that truly matter.
